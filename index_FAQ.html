Please email us at customersrviceheandshe@hotmail.com with your restock request and we

CAN I UPDATE MY ORDER OR MODIFY MY ADDRESS?


For any updates on orders please email us at customerserviceheandshe@hotmail.com

and we will do our best to change or modify your order.


DO YOU ACCEPT FAULTY ITEMS?


If your item is faulty please send us an email with an image to show the faulty item, we

will then issue you with an exchange or store credit if your item is accepted.


CAN I WEAR THE JEWELLERY IN THE SHOWER?


We don't recommend wearing your pieces in the shower, although they are a range of 925 Stirling

silver, 9ct and 14ct gold plated just for longevity and to avoid any issues occurring we ask

that you take them off before showering.


WHAT QUALITY IS YOUR JEWELLERY?


Our pieces range from 925 sterling silver, 9-14ct gold and gold plated.


DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?


At this point in time we don't ship internationally but we are working on having international orders.


DO YOU REFUND?


We do not accept refunds but we do offer exchange or store credit.
