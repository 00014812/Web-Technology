Return Policy

We have a 14 day return policy, which means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request an

exchange or store credit (no refunds)


To be eligible for an exchange or store credit, your item must be in the same condition that you received it,

unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You will also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

A photo of your item must be emailed to customerserviceheandshe@hotmail.com


To start an exchange or store credit, you can contact us at customer service heandshe@hotmail.com


You can always contact us for any queries.


** Disclaimer**

A refund does not exist for customers change of mind on items.


Damages and issues


We offer returns and refunds if the item is faulty. An item is deemed faulty if their is discolouration on material,

rips or holes or missing buttons or embellishments. Please note a faulty item does no consist of a loose thread.


please contact us via our email. The item must be returned to our return address to have this item assessed.


Please note customers are to cover the cost of the return postage. The refund will be processed once the item has

been received on our end and we have reviewed the condition.
